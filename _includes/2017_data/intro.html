About the Book Club

Inspired by the Fall 2018 Collective Courage Online Book Club, this book club will support connection and reflection among anyone who wants to read or re-read Pedagogy of the Oppressed by Paolo Freire.

The group will meet for an hour, once a week, for four consecutive weeks during the Summer of 2018. Exact schedule is TBD, but if you'd like be notified when dates are chosen, submit your contact info below.
